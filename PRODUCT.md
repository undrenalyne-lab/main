# PRODUCT.md — Backchannel Atlas

register: product

## Product purpose

Backchannel Atlas is **Opportunity Intelligence for Global Mobility**: a global engine for economic trajectories with proof, access probability, and executable plans — not a job board or AI country blog.

## Users

People who want to change income, country, or career path without following the classic study → average job → expensive rent loop. Francophones first; architecture supports any origin → destination pair.

## Brand & tone

- Atlas tactique, cockpit cash, anti-bullshit
- Dense, ADHD-friendly, verdict-first
- French UI; global data model
- Never present max verified income as normal or guaranteed

## Strategic principles

1. **Evidence graph** — every big number has source, date, confidence, conditions
2. **Access probability** — separate possibility from likelihood
3. **Corridor intelligence** — product units are origin→destination routes, not countries alone
4. **Narrow wedge, global architecture** — 3 corridors, 30 opportunities, 100 proofs in V1
5. **Monetize decisions** — paid feasibility audit before perfect app

## Anti-references

- Decorative world map with generic AI tips
- Nomad List clone without proof layer
- Visa advice disguised as legal counsel
- "12k/month" without max ≠ average disclaimer

## Primary surfaces

Home (WorldMapHero) → Profile setup → Dashboard (best move) → Country detail → Plan 7/30/90 → Paid audit (external for now)
